About Us

Pakistan Aeronautics is a pioneering aerospace startup dedicated to advancing aerial robotics and defense technologies. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge computational and hardware design solutions for loitering munitions while empowering the next generation through hands-on training and workshops in aerial robotics.

Our Services

Loitering Munitions Design

We specialize in computational and hardware design of advanced loitering munitions, delivering precision-engineered solutions for defense and security applications.

Training & Workshops

Our hands-on training programs and workshops in aerial robotics empower students and professionals at schools, colleges, and universities to excel in aerospace innovation.
